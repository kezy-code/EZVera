Features

Why EZVera?

A modern approach to VeraCrypt volume management

⚡

One-Click Unlock

Save your drives once, unlock them with a single click. No more navigating through menus.

🎯

Auto-Detection

Automatically detects all your disks and partitions, just like VeraCrypt does.

🔒

Secure by Design

Passwords are never stored. Only device paths and mount letters are saved.

🎨

Modern UI

Beautiful, dark-mode interface with smooth animations. A pleasure to use.

🪶

Lightweight

Only ~5MB. Built with Tauri and Rust for maximum performance and minimal footprint.

💻

Cross-Platform

Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Same great experience everywhere.
